Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU)

Thanks to advances in reproductive medicine and pre-natal care, more women today are able to conceive, maintain pregnancies, and deliver babies – even premature and multiple births. Sometimes, however, these babies have special needs that require high quality medical care enhanced with the latest technology. Even when the pregnancy has been problem free, babies can be born with unexpected conditions that require expert care. The St. Luke’s Neonatal Intensive Care Units (NICU) at St. Luke’s Allentown Campus, St. Luke’s University Hospital – Bethlehem and St. Luke’s Anderson Campus are designed to provide a high level of care for babies and their families.
